# Dataset License

The commodity detection dataset in `data/` is released for academic research,
reproducibility, and non-commercial use.

You may use, copy, and redistribute the dataset for academic research if you:

- cite the related manuscript or this repository;
- keep the class names and annotation format unchanged when redistributing;
- do not use the images for commercial product recognition services without
  written permission from the authors.

For additional permission, please contact the corresponding author listed in
the manuscript.
